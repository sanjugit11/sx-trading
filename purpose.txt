🧪 Test/Mock Contracts
Contract	Purpose
MockUSDT	A free-mint ERC-20 token simulating USDT on testnet. Pre-mints 1 billion tokens to the deployer 
and exposes a permissionless mint() function — used as the collateral/settlement currency across all protocol 
contracts.

MockOracle	A simple price feed contract (owner-settable). Stores asset → price mappings. All trading/lending contracts
 query this for current asset prices. Replaces a real Chainlink oracle on testnet.

🏛️ Governance & Safety
Contract	Purpose
SXAdmin	A multisig governance controller. Requires 3 "master devices" (signers). Any privileged action (e.g., pausing trading) 
must go through a createProposal → approveProposal → executeProposal flow requiring unanimous approval. Also controls a kill switch 
that simultaneously pauses SXPT, SXLT, and SXLS.

📈 Core Trading Contracts
Contract	Purpose
SXPT (SX Perpetuals)	The perpetual futures engine (2x–1000x leverage). Users deposit USDT margin to open long/short positions.
 Implements a funding rate mechanism (updated hourly based on long/short open interest skew, capped at 0.3%/hr). Losses are capped at 
 margin (no negative balances). Pausable by SXAdmin.

SXLS (SX Leveraged Spot)	A leveraged spot trading contract (1x–100x). Similar to SXPT but directionally long-only (no shorts).
 Supports limit orders with a trigger price — a keeper can call checkAndExecuteLimitOrder() when the oracle price hits the 
 threshold. Supports take-profit and stop-loss automation. Pausable by SXAdmin.

SXLT (SX Lending Terminal)	A peer-to-pool lending/borrowing protocol. Users can lend assets to earn yield, or borrow against 
collateral at a 250% LTV ratio. Interest rates are dynamic based on pool utilization (2% base + up to 20% at full utilization).
 Repayment accrues time-weighted interest. Pausable by SXAdmin.

🔒 Privacy Trading
Contract	Purpose
SXHOP (SX Hidden Order Protocol)	A commit-reveal hidden order system with mock ZK proof verification. Users submit a keccak256 
commitment hash (hiding their order intent). A keeper later reveals and executes the order — verifying the commitment matches the 
revealed details — then routes it through SXLS. Prevents front-running.

📊 Unified Dashboard
Contract	Purpose
SXUD (SX Unified Dashboard)	A read-only aggregator contract that queries SXPT, SXLT, and SXLS in a single call. Provides: all 
positions/loans in one view (getAllPositions), total cross-product exposure in USD (getTotalExposure), total collateral locked
 (getCrossTerminalCollateral), and a unified risk score (0–100) representing exposure-to-collateral ratio (getUnifiedRiskScore). T
 his is what the backend calls for dashboard metrics.
